Ayodhya 3 Day Tour Package

Ayodhya is a city situated on the banks of the Sarayu river in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh. Ayodhya was historically known as Saketa.

Here are the tour info of two day Ayodhya Tour Package:-

Day One
Pickup from Ayodhya Cantt/Ayodhya railway station. On arrival, check-in at the hotel. After welcome drink, proceed to Visit Shree Swaminarayan Mandir Chhapaiya. En-route visit Makhauda Dham. Evening visit Ram-Ki-Paidi, Later back to the hotel in Ayodhya for night stay.
Day Two
Morning holy dip in Saryu at NayaGhat & visit for darshan of Hanuman Garhi, Ram Janam Bhoomi, Dashrath Bhawan, Kanak Bhawan, Ram Janambhoomi Karyashala, Mani Parvat, Aartiat SaryuGhat & Nageshwar Nath Mandir. Later back to hotel for night stay.
Day Three
Morning visit Raja Dashrath Samadhi Sthal, SurajKund, Nandigram & GuptarGhat. Later drop at Faizabad/Ayodhya Railway station for onward journey.
